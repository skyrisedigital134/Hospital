Welcome to New Prem Ji Hospital

Welcome to our hospital, where compassionate care meets clinical excellence. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare for women, newborns, children, and families in a safe, modern, and patient-centered environment.

Our hospital offers specialized Gynecology and Obstetrics services, including antenatal care, high-risk pregnancy management, normal and cesarean deliveries, infertility evaluation, and open, laparoscopic and hysteroscopic gynecological surgeries — alongside a dedicated Pediatrics department for newborns, infants, children and adolescents.

Our Key Services

A glimpse of the healthcare services available at New Prem Ji Hospital.

Department of Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Treatment for pregnancy, childbirth and women's health conditions.

Non‑descent vaginal hysterectomy

Uterus removed vaginally without prior uterine descent.

Hysteroscopic tubal cannulation

Endoscopic technique to open blocked fallopian tubes.

Neo‑vagina formation (vaginoplasty)

Surgical creation of a functional vaginal canal.

Doppler

A Doppleris a form of ultrasound scan that helps to access your baby's health.

Ultrasound-TA/TVS

A transvaginal ultrasound is a type of pelvic ultrasound used by doctors to examine female reproductive organs.

Department of Pediatrics

Specialists treat sick children and provide preventive medical care from birth through adolescence.

NICU / PICU / ICU

Specialized monitoring and treatment for critically ill patients.

Phototherapy

Phototherapy is a medical treatment using specific light wavelengths to treat skin conditions like psoriasis jaundice in newborns.

Vaccination

Vaccines protect children from dangerous diseases and help them grow up healthy.
