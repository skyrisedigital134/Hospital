Welcome to New Prem Ji Hospital

Welcome to our hospital, where compassionate care meets clinical excellence. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare for women, newborns, children, and families in a safe, modern, and patient-centered environment.

Our hospital offers specialized Gynecology and Obstetrics services, including antenatal care, high-risk pregnancy management, normal and cesarean deliveries, infertility evaluation, and open, laparoscopic and hysteroscopic gynecological surgeries — alongside a dedicated Pediatrics department for newborns, infants, children and adolescents.
